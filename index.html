Manual Guide

A) Check your oven balance

Go to the storage explorer on better-call.dev ↗ and locate your oven in the ovens bigmap by searching for your Tezos address. Make note of the oven ID, ctez_outstanding, and tez_balance.

B) Close one oven in a batch

  1. Create a batch operation on the contract interaction page on better-call.dev ↗.
  2. If the oven has ctez outstanding, add mint_or_burn first:
    • id: Your oven ID
    • quantity: The negative of your ctez_outstanding balance (e.g. -100000000)
  3. Add withdraw second when the oven has tez:
    • id: Your oven ID
    • amount: Your full tez_balance in mutez
    • to: Your wallet address
  4. Submit the batch. If either operation is wrong, the whole batch fails.
