Manual Guide

A) Check your oven balance

Go to the storage explorer on better-call.dev ↗ and locate your oven in the ovens bigmap by searching for your Tezos address. Make note of the oven ID, ctez_outstanding, and tez_balance.

B) Burn all ctez

  1. Go to the mint_or_burn interaction page on better-call.dev ↗.
  2. Fill in:
    • id: Your oven ID
    • quantity: Negative value equal to your full ctez_outstanding (e.g. -100000000)
  3. Connect wallet and submit.

C) Withdraw all tez

  1. Stay on the same better-call.dev interact page.
  2. Select entrypoint withdraw.
  3. Fill in:
    • id: Your oven ID
    • amount: Your full tez_balance in mutez (try slightly smaller if it fails)
    • to: Your wallet address
  4. Submit.
