A) Check your oven balance

Go to https://better-call.dev/mainnet/KT1GWnsoFZVHGh7roXEER3qeCcgJgrXT3de2/storage and find your oven in the ovens bigmap by entering your address in the search field. Note oven id, ctez_outstanding, and tez_balance.

B) Burn all ctez

1. Go to https://better-call.dev/mainnet/KT1GWnsoFZVHGh7roXEER3qeCcgJgrXT3de2/interact/mint_or_burn
2. Fill in:
   - id — your oven ID
   - quantity — negative value equal to your full ctez_outstanding (e.g. -100000000)
3. Connect wallet and submit

C) Withdraw all tez

1. Stay on the same better-call.dev interact page
2. Select entrypoint `withdraw`
3. Fill in:
   - id — your oven ID
   - amount — your full tez_balance in mutez (try slightly smaller if fails)
   - to — your wallet address
4. Submit
